Thesis

I am currently looking for a new adventure after my graduation from Malmö University as an Interaction Designer. During my studies, I have not only focused on interaction design, but I have also taken on multiple courses on subjects such as project leading, digital innovation, and web development. Through my education, I have studied and learned the principles behind user-centered design, the importance of teamwork, collecting requirements, interpreting requirements, and meeting deadlines.

I have work experience as a machine operator from the production at Trioworld AB in Landskrona. It was here my interest in usability was born, as I was constantly trying to figure out how I could improve the systems and processes from a usability perspective for us on the production line. This experience is why I chose to study interaction design, as I released how much that could be improved by understanding the end-users experience of utilizing the product, system, or service which you have created.

As a person, I am curious, creative, solution-orientated, and detail-oriented. I work great in groups and like to exchange ideas with co-workers. I often take the initiative and contribute with suggestions on how we might work with some areas of improvement.
